## 3. TDR (TRANSFERABLE DEVELOPMENT RIGHTS) ECONOMICS — THE SCARCITY PROOF

TDR is the regulatory mechanism that forces new FD developers to pay up for FSI because the area is land-locked and core densities are capped.

### How TDR translates to project cost (per-sqft impact)

| Location | Extra cost per sqft from TDR (before GST/lending) | Net per-sqft TDR burden |
|----------|---------------------------------------------------|------------------------|
| Nanakramguda (FD core) | ₹399/sqft | **₹551/sqft** |
| Khajaguda | ₹278/sqft | ₹384/sqft |
| Kukatpally | ₹216/sqft | ₹297/sqft |
| Kondapur | ₹234/sqft | ₹322/sqft |
| Miyapur | ₹198/sqft | ₹273/sqft |
| Manchirevula | ₹198/sqft | ₹274/sqft |
| Puppalguda | ₹169/sqft | ₹233/sqft |
| Attapur | ₹161/sqft | ₹222/sqft |
| Nallagandla | ₹140/sqft | ₹193/sqft |
| Kokapet | ₹131/sqft | ₹181/sqft |
| Neopolis | ₹128/sqft | ₹177/sqft |
| Tellapur | ₹127/sqft | ₹175/sqft |
| Narsingi | ₹113/sqft | ₹155/sqft |

### What this means for the sales narrative
A new FD tower requires the developer to absorb **₹551/sqft of TDR cost** (Nanakramguda benchmark) on top of land + construction + margin. Loft locked its FSI at project launch (Aug 2023). **The next FD launch will structurally price 15-20% above Loft's current ticket** because TDR + land costs have only moved upward.

**Use this fact for:** resale logic, "wait for price drop" objection, location defense vs cheaper micro-markets.

## 5. INFRASTRUCTURE CATALYSTS (confidence multipliers, not price-jump promises)

### Metro Phase II (proposed, 76.4 km)
Proposed expansion of Hyderabad Metro. Timeline and final routes are subject to government execution. Frame as a **confidence multiplier**, not a pricing guarantee.

### Water Supply Expansion (₹8,858 Cr mega-project)
- Godavari Phase II+III: ₹7,360 Cr to bring 20 TMC from Mallanna Sagar (17.5 TMC for Hyderabad drinking water)
- ORR Phase 2: ₹1,200 Cr, 71 reservoirs (15 ready), serving 25 lakh people in 14 mandals
- Kokapet project: ₹298 Cr for Neopolis, SEZ, and Kokapet Layout (13 lakh people)
- **Net impact:** Hyderabad water supply rising from 580-600 MGD to ~880 MGD in 2 years (~50% increase)
- Foundation laid 2025; 24-month completion target

### Flyovers & Road Work (H-CITI project)
- GHMC building 2 new multi-level flyovers on Mehdipatnam–Gachibowli stretch (₹398 Cr)
- Trumpet Interchange (Exit-1A) at Neopolis on ORR — completed and inaugurated 2025
- Multiple ROB works between exit 2 and Gaudium being executed

### Airport
Rajiv Gandhi International Airport — 35 min from Loft. Continued expansion as Hyderabad's primary air hub.
